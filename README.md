# Tangible for Claude

**Run real customer research without leaving Claude.**

Tangible drafts a custom research study in about **3 minutes** and recruits real respondents in **24–72 hours**. This plugin connects Claude to Tangible, so you can create a study, check on it, and read the results — all in chat.

## Install

Find **Tangible** in the Claude Code plugin directory (`/plugin` → Discover), or install from the community marketplace:

```
/plugin marketplace add anthropics/claude-plugins-community
/plugin install tangible-for-claude@claude-community
```

That's it — there's no separate login step. The first time you use Tangible, Claude opens the sign-in page in your browser; finish signing in there and you're connected.

## What you can do

Just ask in plain English — Claude picks the right workflow:

| Say something like… | What happens |
|---|---|
| *"I want to run a study"* | Claude walks you through picking a study type, describing your audience in your own words, and a few quick questions — then Tangible drafts the full study for you. |
| *"Show me my studies"* / *"what's running?"* | A tidy status view of your studies: what's drafting, collecting responses, or finished. |
| *"Change the audience to enterprise only"* | Edit any draft (or live study) with a plain-English instruction — the whole study updates consistently. |
| *"What did we learn?"* / *"which concept won?"* | Claude reads the results — the verdict, key themes, and real respondent quotes — and explains them, grounded in the actual data. |
| *"Am I connected?"* | A quick connection check, plus first-time setup if you're new. |

## Study types

| Study type | Answers |
|---|---|
| **Win / Loss** | Why did we win or lose these deals — beyond CRM "closed-lost: competitor". |
| **Message Test** | Which of 2–5 taglines / value props / short copy variants lands. |
| **Concept Test** | Which of 2–5 landing pages, ads, designs, packaging, or feature mockups wins. |
| **Brand Perception** | How your brand stacks up against named competitors. |
| **Churn Diagnosis** | Why customers actually cancelled, downgraded, or didn't renew. |
| **Ask a Customer** | Open-ended voice-of-customer — runs as a quick survey or a short AI voice interview. |

Pricing appears on the study's review page in the Tangible app once your draft is ready — you'll always see the cost before you launch.

## How it works

1. **Describe your problem** — "we lost three big deals last quarter and I don't know why."
2. **Answer a short intake** — audience in your own words, plus a couple of specifics.
3. **Tangible drafts the study** — brief, questionnaire, and audience targeting, in ~3 minutes.
4. **Review and launch** — check the draft in chat, tweak anything, then launch from the Tangible app.
5. **Get answers** — respondents are recruited in 24–72 hours; ask Claude what the results mean.

## Good to know

- **Launching happens in the Tangible app** — Claude drafts and edits, but you always review and confirm the launch (and pricing) in your browser.
- **Your data stays yours** — respondent personal details are redacted before anything reaches Claude.
- **Not the right fit if** you need a fully custom 60-question instrument, multi-wave brand tracking, or researcher-grade methodology controls — Tangible Studio (coming soon) handles those.
